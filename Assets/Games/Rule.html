Play these 5 games and win it.

Winning Each game will give a code piece.

Rearrange the pieces and you get the secret code.


Winning Criteria for each game :

2048 : Score 2500 or above. (Use arrow keys to move tiles).

Dino : Score 250 or above. (Use space to jump)

Matching : Win with less than 30 flips. (Mouse Clicks)

Snake : Score 100 or above. (Use arrow keys to move snake)

Wordle : Just Win the Game. (Type word in the blank space and press enter)
